Trades

Published: 28 Jul 2024 | Updated: 28 Jul 2024

Herein you'll find the details you'll need if you'd like to trade (digitally or by mail) with me! I love swapping and meeting new folks, so please, if you'd like to swap... Onwards!

I can offer: ATCs, book recommendations, collages, doodles, floral/plant themed ephemera, handmade envelopes, letters, my zines, playlists, and written postcards.

Always happy to receive: ATCs, blank or written postcards, comics, drawings, ephemera, stickers, maps, pamphlets, pin buttons, poetry, pressed leaves/flowers, washi tape, and zines.

Not looking for: CDs, DVDs, fabric, plants, patches, print books or photos, seeds, thread, vinyl records, or yarn.

Interested? Please, email me!
